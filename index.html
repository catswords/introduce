Namhyeon Go (Catswords)

Hello, I’m Namhyeon Go, leading a one-person software team called Catswords in South Korea.

I develop and share a wide range of open-source projects on GitHub, including frameworks, middleware, and debugging tools. My currently active projects include ReasonableFramework (based on PHP), WelsonJS (built on Windows ECMAScript with C#), and Caterpillar Proxy (based on Python). Recently, I’ve been actively exploring and contributing to open technologies and protocols like WebAssembly and ActivityPub.

My mission is to ensure these technologies are widely accessible to everyone, free from any organizational or financial constraints, through my open-source work.

Sponsorship plays a crucial role in maintaining and improving the security and quality of my open-source projects, while also ensuring that I remain independent from any specific organizations. It allows me to stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends and ensure my projects evolve to meet changing demands. Additionally, sponsorship helps me engage with a broader community.

If you’re interested in discussing alternative ways to sponsor or if you are looking to hire me part-time or full-time, feel free to contact me.
